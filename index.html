Our Story

Built on Craftsmanship,
Driven by Detail

For over two decades, J&P Kitchen & Bath has been transforming homes across the Greater Toronto Area. Based in Scarborough, we combine old-world craftsmanship with modern design sensibilities to create spaces that are as functional as they are beautiful.

Every project — from a single bathroom refresh to a full kitchen overhaul — receives our complete attention. We work closely with each client to understand their vision, their lifestyle, and their budget, then deliver results that exceed expectations.
